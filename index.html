Z Σ P T Λ R

A product designed to power up/charge the wireless mouse without having the pain to plug it in an outlet. The very purpose of this project is to create a very comfortable experience for the people who will use it like students, workers, and gamers where the won’t to be bothered by plugging everything and lesser wires to deal with. It is also our goal to be able to introduce but more like encourage people to be more familiar with wireless charging to be able to improve this field.

WIRELESS CAPABLE MOUSEPAD

With wireless charging system, ZEPTAR, we have removed the thing called ‘cable’ in “wireless” mouse. We wanted to create a full-on wireless mouse that can get rid of cables. Due to ZEPTAR, we are able to solve the battery barrier, now, dead batteries are gone, infinite charging is here.

WIRELESS TECHNOLOGY

ZEPTAR Provides a wireless transmitter to Charge compativle device (Any device with wireless receiver) which will deliver Endless times of charging when placed on the Pad. This will charge your mouse while you are using it, without wires.
